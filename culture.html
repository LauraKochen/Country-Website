Culture

Culture in Australia, deeply rooted in diversity, shapes its identity.

Uluru, a sacred monolith in Australia's heart, holds profound significance for Aboriginal peoples. Known as Uluru to the Anangu, it embodies ancestral stories, spiritual connections, and cultural identity. It serves as a living symbol of creation, guiding traditions, and fostering a deep sense of belonging and reverence within Aboriginal communities.
